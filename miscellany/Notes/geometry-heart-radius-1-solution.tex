Get the equations:
\begin{align*}
\left(r-\frac{1}{2}\right)^2 
  & = \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^2
    + \left(\sqrt{2}-r\right)^2
    - 2 \left(\frac{1}{2}\right) (\sqrt{2}-r) \cos{\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right)} \\
\Rightarrow \quad 
r & = \frac{2}{3\sqrt{2}-2} \\[2ex]
r & = \frac{2(3\sqrt{2}+2)}{(3\sqrt{2}-2)(3\sqrt{2}+2)} \\[2ex]
r & = \frac{3\sqrt{2}+2}{7} 
\end{align*}
