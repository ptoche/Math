Convert $100_{b+1}$ to base $b$, where $b\ge3$.
\begin{align*}
100_{b+1} 
= 1 \mult (b+1)^{2} + 0 \mult (b+1)^{1} + 0 \mult (b+1)^{0}
= b^{2} + 2b + 1
= 1 \mult b^{2} + 2 \mult b^{1} + 1 \mult b^{0}
\to 121_{b}
\end{align*}
If $b<3$, the factor $2$ in front of $b^{2}$ would unravel. 

Solution: \fbox{$121_{b}$.}
