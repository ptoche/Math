\begin{align*}
3 \mult 4^{0} 
& = 3
\\
4^{2} - 3 \mult 4^{1} 
& = 4
\\
3 \mult 4^{2} + 3 \mult 4^{0} 
& = 51
\\
4^{4} - 3 \mult 4^{3} - 3 \mult 4 
& = 52
\\
3 \mult 4^{4} + 3 \mult 4^{2} + 3 \mult 4^{0} 
& = 819
\\
4^{6} - 3 \mult 4^{5} - 3 \mult 4^{3} 
& = 820
\\
3 \mult 4^{6} + 3 \mult 4^{4} + 3 \mult 4^{2} + 3 \mult 4^{0} 
& = 13827
% 3 * 4**6 + 3 * 4**4 + 3 * 4**4 + 3
\end{align*}

Adding up the $4$ digits weighted by the number of cases gives:
\begin{align*}
\sum_{i=1}^{2013} b(i)
& = 1 \mult (3-1+1)
  + 3 \mult (51-4+1)
  + 5 \mult (819-52+1)
  + 7 \mult (2013-820+1)
\\
& = 3 + 48 + 768 + 1195
  = 12345
% 1 * (3-1+1) + 3 * (51-4+1) + 5 * (819-52+1) + 7 * (2013-820+1)
\end{align*}

Solution: \fbox{$12345$.}
